Trading Education

What are Options?

Options are financial contracts that give you the right, but not the obligation, to buy or sell cryptocurrency at a specific price within a certain time period.

Call Options: Bet on price going up
Put Options: Bet on price going down

How to Play

  1. Analyze the current cryptocurrency market conditions
  2. Choose from available call or put options
  3. Place your trade with virtual currency
  4. Wait for the expiration time to see results
  5. Learn from your wins and losses!

Trading Glossary

Strike Price

The price at which the option can be exercised

Expiration

The time when the option contract expires

Premium

The cost to buy the option contract

In the Money

When the option has intrinsic value
